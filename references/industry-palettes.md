# Industry palettes

Use these as anchor suggestions. Pick 2-3 anchors, then derive roles.

## Beverage - coconut

- Coconut Cream #F7F2E8 (base or background)
- Palm Leaf #3E6B4E (brand or headings)
- Seafoam Splash #7BC9B2 (accent or CTA)
- Sand Beige #D9CBB6 (surface)
- Driftwood #8B7A62 (text or muted)

## Beverage - juice

- Citrus Peel #F4B53F (energy or accent)
- Guava Pink #E77B8A (highlight)
- Lime Leaf #6FBF66 (freshness)
- Cloud White #F9F7F2 (background)
- Cocoa Husk #5B4C43 (text)

## Beverage - coffee

- Espresso #3B2A22 (primary or text)
- Crema #E8D7C0 (background)
- Amber Roast #B8703C (accent)
- Latte Foam #F4EFE7 (surface)
- Ash Brown #7A6A5E (muted)

## Beverage - tea

- Matcha Leaf #5F7F55 (primary)
- Jasmine #F5F2E8 (background)
- Oolong #A88E6B (secondary)
- Citrus Zest #E4B653 (accent)
- Ink #403830 (text)

## General fallback

- Use product cues (ingredient, packaging, origin) to pick anchors.
- Prefer 2-3 anchors, then add a neutral ramp and one accent.
